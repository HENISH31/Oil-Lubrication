Why Viscosity Matters

Viscosity is the measure of an oil's resistance to flow. It changes with temperature, affecting how well your engine is protected during starts and high-speed runs.

The "W" Number (0W)

Represents winter/cold flow. The lower the number, the faster oil reaches critical parts during a cold start.

The Hot Number (40)

Represents protection at operating temp. Higher numbers offer thicker film strength for heavy loads.
